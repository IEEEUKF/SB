Why AKIASSC?

Workshops

There are three parallel events associated with AKIASSC 2020. The participants have the option of selecting the workshop as per their priority. Three workshops are arranged for giving opportunity to participants to choose worshop on their interested topic





Visits

Industrial visit is the one of the top attraction of the event. It will help the participants to get updated with recent technologies in industries.It will improve the practical knowledge of the participants.

Talks

There are various kinds of talks associated with AKIASSC 2020. It includes technical talks, motivational talks, Panel disscussion and also talks on entrepreneurship. The technical talks are on various relevant topics related to most latest technologies in industries. Motivational talk is to ignite the minds of young vibrant participants.Panel discussion is a platform for the participants to clear their doubts related to technological advancements in industries.
